Within

Within is a virtual/ augmented reality entertainment company that focuses on giving VR storytellers a platform to showcase their work. This redesign will explore other homepage design options to enhance their customer’s immersive experience.

We are not afiliated with Within and are doing this study for research purposes only.

The objective of this website re-design was to enhance the user experience and brand’s message on Within’s homepage by highlighting the VR headset. The re-design included desktop and mobile unique idea sketch sets, logo and any other branding that the company uses.

Research: Our research consisted of doing a comparative analysis of competitor sites such as Jaunt VR, The Virtual Reality Company, and other various virtual and augmented reality company sites. Currently, virtual reality headsets are still considered a niche market because of price, limited virtual outlets, content, and lack of mass consumer interest, but it’s slowly growing in the entertainment industry. More VR companies are trying to create mobile-friendly viewing experiences to bridge the gap between consumers that own VR headsets and ones that don’t.

Audience: The primary target audience is early tech adopters or people interested in the virtual reality cinematic experience. Consumers can watch films on Within using the company’s video player, mobile phones or premium VR headsets.
